Sir Timothy John Berners-Lee

also known as TimBL, is an English computer scientist best known as the inventor of the World Wide Web, HTML, the URL system, and HTTP

About him

He is a professorial research fellow at the University of Oxford and a professor emeritus at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT). He is the director of the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C), which oversees the continued development of the Web. He is also the founder of the World Wide Web Foundation and a senior researcher and holder of the 3Com Founders Chair at the Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence Laboratory (CSAIL) at MIT.

Why I Admire Him

I admire Sir Timothy John Berners-Lee because he invented the World Wide Web and chose to share it freely with the world instead of profiting from it. His work transformed how people communicate, learn, and access sinformation, making knowledge more open and accessible to everyone. Beyond his invention, he continues to advocate for a fair, safe, and open internet, showing humility, integrity, and a deep commitment to using technology for the good of humanity.
